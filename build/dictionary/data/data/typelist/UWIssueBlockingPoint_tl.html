UWIssueBlockingPoint     (pctl_uwissueblockingpoint)
The points at which a UWIssue can block progress of a job. When checking against a particular blocking point, any issues with a higher priority will be considered to also block, i.e. issues that block at quote will also block at bind. NonBlocking must always remain at priority 0.
